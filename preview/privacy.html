Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Tan Bomb Ltd (“Tan Bomb”, “we”, “us” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect when you use www.tan-bomb.com, how we use it, and your rights under UK data protection law (including UK GDPR).

1. Who we are

Tan Bomb Ltd sells tanning products online, including Cherry Whip.

Email: info@tan-bomb.com
Website: www.tan-bomb.com

2. What data we collect

Depending on how you use the site, we may collect:

  • Order details — name, email, phone number, billing and shipping address, and payment status when you buy from us
  • Contact details — name, email and message content if you contact us (for example via the contact form or email)
  • Technical data — basic device/browser information and logs needed to run and secure the website (for example IP address, pages visited)

We do not store your full card details. Payments are processed by Stripe.

3. How we use your data

We use your personal data to:

  • Process and fulfil orders
  • Send order confirmations and receipts (via Stripe and/or us)
  • Respond to enquiries from customers, salons and stockists
  • Improve and secure our website
  • Meet legal and accounting requirements

Our legal bases are typically: performance of a contract (orders), legitimate interests (running and improving the business, security), and legal obligation (records we must keep).

4. Payments (Stripe)

Checkout and card payments are handled by Stripe. When you pay, Stripe processes your payment information. Stripe may also send you a payment receipt or invoice email.

See Stripe’s privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

5. Sharing your data

We only share personal data when needed to run the business, for example with:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Hosting providers — such as Vercel, which hosts this website
  • Delivery / fulfilment partners — if needed to ship your order
  • Professional advisers — for example accountants or legal advisers, where required

We do not sell your personal data.

6. Cookies and analytics

Our site uses essential cookies/technologies needed for basic site function (for example checkout and security). We do not use advertising cookies to track you across other sites.

If we add analytics tools later, we will update this policy and, where required, ask for consent.

7. How long we keep data

We keep order and transaction records for as long as needed for fulfilment, customer support, and legal/tax requirements. Contact messages are kept only as long as needed to respond and follow up. We then delete or anonymise data where we no longer need it.

8. International transfers

Some providers (for example Stripe or hosting services) may process data outside the UK. Where that happens, appropriate safeguards are used as required by UK law.

9. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete data (in certain cases)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability (in certain cases)
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, email info@tan-bomb.com. You can also complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO): ico.org.uk.

10. Children

Our products and website are not aimed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Please check this page periodically.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data:
